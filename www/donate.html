Donate!

OpenDKIM is freeware. It's maintained by a small group of people that dedicate their spare time and energy to keep the project alive, provide support, fix bugs, write documentation, advance the relevant standards, and keep current with trends in the industry.

If you benefit from using the software, either personally or professionally, you might consider making a donation to cover the time and costs of the developers.

We thank you for your support!
